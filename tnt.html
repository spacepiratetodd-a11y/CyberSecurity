CyberSecurity For Everyday Life

Here are 10 easy-to-follow cybersecurity tips anyone can use to stay safer online.

    🔐 Use Strong, Unique Passwords. Never reuse passwords from site to site. Use a password manager to generate unique passwords

    📲 Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA). When a site asks, no matter how inconvenient it seems, use it

    🧼 Keep Software Updated. Up to date software will usually contain the most up to date security measures

    📧 Be Wary of Phishing Emails. If you're not expecting it, it's probably not legit. Banks and other companies will NOT ask for login info

    🛡️ Use Antivirus and Firewall Protection. There are many cheap and free antivirus softwares available

    🌐 Avoid Public Wi-Fi for Sensitive Tasks. If you're out in public, use your vpn

    🧠 Think Before You Share. Know where you are and who you're sharing your information with

    🧹 Clean Up Old Accounts. Delete old accounts and be aware of where your information may be posted

    🧩 Check App Permissions. Your apps don't need most permissions to operate, most are for data collection and can put you at risk

    🔍 Monitor Your Accounts. Regularly check your email and bankstatements
